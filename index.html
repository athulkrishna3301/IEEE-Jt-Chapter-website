About Our Chapter

IEEE PHO/MTT-S/SSCS/SPS Jt Chapter CEK consists of 4 IEEE Technical Societies IEEE Photonics Society,IEEE Solid State Circuit Society,IEEE Microwave Theory and Technological Society,IEEE Signal Processing Society.

IEEE PHO

Photonics Society — light-based technologies, lasers, and optical communications
IEEE MTTS

Microwave Theory & Techniques — RF, wireless, antennas, and microwave systems
IEEE SSCS

Solid-State Circuits — microchips, integrated circuits, and electronics design
IEEE SPS

Signal Processing — algorithms, audio, image, and data processing
